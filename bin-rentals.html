Service agreements

  • Red brick will be dumped at a rate of $70/ton

  • Fuel surcharge will be applied, this rate fluctuates monthly based on the previous month average at the Natural Resources Canada website.

  • Garbage/Refuse waste may be household waste or construction waste only. Hazardous materials are not allowed such as paint, chemicals, liquids, tires, propane tanks, medical or radioactive material. Bins containing any of these materials may be rejected from pickup or additional charges may apply to cover cost of appropriate disposal.

  • Clean fill bins may not contain any garbage whatsoever. Clean fill may consist of clean dirt, topsoil, grass or sod. Extra charges may apply if these guidelines are not followed.

  • Concrete bins and asphalt bins may not be mixed unless otherwise arranged with dispatch at the time of ordering. Concrete and asphalt bins may not contain any garbage or dirt. Extra charges may apply if these guidelines are not followed.

  • Although we will do our best to protect client's property from damage by laying protective material under the bin, we do not accept responsibility for damaged driveway's.
